lamp
The lamp uses 20awg brass wire as a frame and for the circuit
The ic's used are a 8-bit shift register and an op amp
Two op amps are used to create two clocks at about 1hz and 7hz
Diodes are used to set the duty cycle

One led is lit up and moves in a circle around the lamp

I plan to redo the base of the lamp with wood
